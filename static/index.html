    🔐 EIP-7702 Delegation Checker — After the Pectra upgrade (2025), EIP-7702 lets smart contracts "borrow" your EOA wallet to sign transactions on your behalf.
    ⚠️ Attack vector: You click "Connect Wallet" on a shady site → sign an innocent-looking signature → the contract gets full delegation over your wallet → drains all tokens without your private key.
    🧠 Many users don't know their wallet is delegated. This checker scans 5 chains (ETH, BSC, Base, Arbitrum, Polygon) simultaneously — if 0xef01... code appears in your wallet, you have an active delegation.
