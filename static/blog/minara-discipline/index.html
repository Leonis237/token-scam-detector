Deep-dive: The V10 6-Layer System

Each layer acts as a shield to filter out market "noise":

  1. Layer 1 Directional Momentum (+DI > -DI): Bulls in control.
  2. Layer 2 Trend Strength (ADX > ADX Mean 9): Ensures the trend is actually gaining strength.
  3. Layer 3 Short-term Trend (Price > EMA63): Guarantees short-term momentum.
  4. Layer 4 Macro Trend Filter (MVP — Price > EMA4800): The "Holy Grail." This single layer saved V10 from ~20 false breakouts during the brutal 2025 chop phase.
  5. Layer 5 Breakout Confirmation (Close > Previous High): Confirmed by actual price action.
  6. Layer 6 Trend Separation (Gap EMA2400/4800 > 2.2x ATR): Avoids risky, narrow consolidation zones.
